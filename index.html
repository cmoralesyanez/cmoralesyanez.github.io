Geophysicist · Ph.D. in Earth & Planetary Sciences

Catalina Morales-Yáñez

Assistant Professor, Departamento de Geofísica — Universidad de Concepción

Postdoctoral FONDECYT Fellow

I'm a Doctor in Earth and Planetary Sciences specializing in seismology. My work focuses on seismology and seismic simulation, using Bayesian transdimensional methods to characterize earthquake sources, b-value variability, and slow slip. I have extensive experience in fieldwork, teaching diverse audiences, and collaborating with multidisciplinary international teams.

I recently joined the Geophysics Department at the Universidad de Concepción and am seeking motivated students to join my research team — feel free to email me.
