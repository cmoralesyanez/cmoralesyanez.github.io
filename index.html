Catalina Morales-Yáñez

Geophysicist, Ph.D.

Assistant Professor at Universidad de Concepción

Postdoc FONDECYT fellow

I'm a Doctor in Earth and Planetary Sciences who specializes in Seismology. Throughout my career, I have developed expertise in various research areas within geophysics, with a main focus on seismology and seismic simulation. I have extensive experience in fieldwork, teaching diverse audiences, and collaborating with multidisciplinary teams.

I recently joined the Geophysics Department at the Universidad de Concepción and am seeking motivated students to join my research team. If you are interested, please feel free to email me.
